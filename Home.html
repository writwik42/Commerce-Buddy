About Commerce Buddy

Commerce Buddy is a next-generation learning platform built to transform the way students understand commerce. Designed specifically for Class 11 and 12 students across WBCHSE, CBSE, and ICSE boards, our platform focuses on simplifying complex concepts and building a strong academic foundation.

Founded by a finance professional with over five years of experience in the Indian accounting and audit domain, along with exposure to global financial reporting frameworks such as US GAAP and IFRS, Commerce Buddy bridges the gap between theoretical knowledge and real-world application.

We believe commerce is not just about scoring marks—it is about understanding how businesses work, how financial decisions are made, and how the economy functions in real life.

Our Focus:
  • Concept Clarity – Simple structured explanations
  • Exam Readiness – MCQs aligned with WBCHSE pattern
  • Real-World Learning – Practical business examples
  • Skill Development – Analytical & financial thinking
  • Comprehensive Coverage – Class 11 & 12 syllabus
