About Commerce Buddy

Commerce Buddy is a modern learning platform designed to simplify commerce education for students of Class 11 and 12 under WBCHSE, CBSE, and ICSE boards.

Founded by a working professional with over 4 years of experience in Indian accounting and audit, including global reporting frameworks (US GAAP, IFRS), Commerce Buddy bridges the gap between academics and real-world application.

Our aim is not just better grades, but strong conceptual clarity and practical understanding that prepares students for real careers.

Our Focus:
  • Clear, concise commerce notes and explanations
  • Exam-ready practice (MCQs for WBCHSE semester exams)
  • Skills development and career guidance
  • Coverage of key topics across Class 11 and 12

Our Mission & Vision

🎯 Our Mission

To make commerce education simple, practical, and accessible, while helping students build strong conceptual clarity and confidence.

🌍 Our Vision

To become a trusted commerce learning brand that goes beyond textbooks by developing real skills, job readiness, and guiding students toward smarter career choices.
