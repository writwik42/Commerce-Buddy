How to Study Accountancy in Class 11 (Step-by-Step Guide)

Accountancy is one of the most important subjects in Class 11 Commerce. Many students find it difficult at first, but with the right approach, it becomes one of the easiest and scoring subjects.

Why Students Find Accountancy Difficult

Many students struggle because they try to memorize journal entries instead of understanding the logic behind transactions. Accountancy is not about memory — it is about understanding the flow of money in a business.

1. Understand the Basics First

Before jumping into journal entries, make sure you understand basic concepts like assets, liabilities, and capital. These form the foundation of accountancy.

2. Practice Daily

Accountancy is a practical subject. The more you practice, the better you understand. Try solving at least 5–10 questions daily.

3. Focus on Concepts, Not Memorization

Instead of memorizing entries, understand why a transaction is recorded in a certain way. This helps in solving any type of question.

4. Revise Regularly

Make short notes and revise them weekly. This will help you retain concepts for a longer time.

5. Learn with Real-Life Examples

Try to relate concepts with real business situations. This makes learning easier and more interesting.

Conclusion

If you follow the right strategy and stay consistent, Accountancy can become your strongest subject. Focus on clarity, practice regularly, and build confidence.

Pro Tip from Commerce Buddy

Focus on clarity, not speed. Once your concepts are clear, speed will automatically improve. Always try to connect theory with real-life examples.
