Accountancy

Fundamentals of Accounting (Semester 1)

  • Which financial statement shows a company’s financial position at a given date?

    Answer: The Balance Sheet.

  • What is the accounting equation?

    Answer: Assets = Liabilities + Equity.

Accounting Standards (Semester 2)

  • Which global framework applies to multinational companies?

    Answer: IFRS (International Financial Reporting Standards).

Business Studies

Business Environment (Semester 1)

  • What is meant by 'Globalization' in business?

    Answer: The increasing integration and interdependence of national economies.

Business Finance (Semester 2)

  • What is the capital structure of a company?

    Answer: The mix of debt and equity financing used by a firm.

Economics

Microeconomics (Semester 1)

  • What is the law of demand?

    Answer: As price decreases, quantity demanded increases (ceteris paribus).

Macroeconomics (Semester 2)

  • What does GDP stand for?

    Answer: Gross Domestic Product.
