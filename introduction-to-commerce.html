Introduction to Commerce: Complete Beginner Guide for Class 11

Commerce is not just a subject—it is a practical life skill that helps us understand how the business world works and how money flows in the economy.

Simple Meaning: Commerce includes all activities involved in the exchange of goods and services.

Meaning & Purpose of Commerce
