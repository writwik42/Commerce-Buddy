🌍 Commerce: The Bridge Between Business and Daily Life

Commerce isn’t just a subject—it’s the backbone of trade, finance, and management. It connects business operations with financial decision-making and builds real-world understanding.

📚 The Subjects That Shape Commerce

  • Accounting – Recording transactions and financial results.
  • Business Studies – Understanding management and operations.
  • Cost Accounting – Controlling production costs.
  • Taxation – Understanding tax rules.
  • Commercial Law – Legal business framework.
  • Auditing – Verifying accounts.
  • Economics – Understanding markets and resources.
